# Shreds

Shreds is a MiniPay-native discovery experience built on Celo, where users slash open digital packs to discover stablecoin rewards, collectible cards, and educational facts about the Celo ecosystem.

Instead of simply claiming rewards, every pack becomes a moment of discovery.

Users swipe through premium packs, slash across the selected pack, watch it tear open, and uncover real rewards alongside collectible discoveries.


# Why Shreds?

Shreds transforms rewards into an engaging discovery experience.

Every pack can reveal:

- USDM
- USDT on Celo
- Collectible Discovery Cards
- "Did You Know?" Cards
- Shred Facts about Celo
- Shred Facts about MiniPay
- XP and Achievements

Every discovery is permanently added to the user's collection.



# How It Works

1. Open Shreds.
2. MiniPay connects automatically.
3. Swipe between available packs.
4. Slash across a pack to shred it open.
5. Discover rewards and collectibles.
6. Build your collection.
7. Earn Shred XP and climb the leaderboard.
8. Return to discover something new.
